# Modeling Principles

1. Pillar pages are central explanatory maps, not only long articles.
2. Topic clusters provide depth around a central subject.
3. Internal links are knowledge pathways and should be meaningful.
4. Cluster articles should have distinct roles.
5. Metadata makes the architecture governable.
6. Coverage should include foundations, methods, applications, critique, and governance.
7. Search visibility should not replace conceptual coherence.
8. AI-assisted mapping requires human validation.
9. Pillar-cluster systems should be audited as graphs.
10. Governance prevents content drift, link decay, and metadata inconsistency.
