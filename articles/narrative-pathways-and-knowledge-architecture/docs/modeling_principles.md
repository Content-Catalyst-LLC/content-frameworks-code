# Modeling Principles

1. Readers experience knowledge over time.
2. Narrative pathways connect reader states, article roles, sequence, transition, and orientation.
3. Previous/next navigation is useful but not sufficient for pathway design.
4. Internal links are narrative infrastructure, not only technical SEO.
5. Bridge quality matters as much as link presence.
6. Reader states should shape pathway design.
7. Pathways can be foundational, learning-oriented, methodological, research-oriented, strategic, critical, exploratory, or governance-focused.
8. Pathways should be auditable through metadata.
9. AI-assisted pathway suggestions require human editorial review.
10. Governance prevents pathway drift as the publication grows.
