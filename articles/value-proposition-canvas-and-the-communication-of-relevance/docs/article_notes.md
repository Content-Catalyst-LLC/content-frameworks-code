# Article Notes

**Value Proposition Canvas and the Communication of Relevance** follows **Jobs to Be Done and the Problem of Audience Need** and precedes **STP: Segmentation, Targeting, and Positioning** in the Content Frameworks article map.

The article treats the Value Proposition Canvas as a communication framework, not just a business worksheet. The central editorial question is whether a message credibly connects an audience situation to a proposed form of value.
