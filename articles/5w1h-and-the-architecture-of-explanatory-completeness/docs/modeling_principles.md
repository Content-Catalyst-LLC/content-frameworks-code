# Modeling Principles

1. 5W1H is an explanatory-completeness framework, not merely a checklist.
2. Every answered question should have appropriate support.
3. Completeness is purpose-dependent; not every article needs equal weight for all six questions.
4. Missing questions should be answered, linked elsewhere, or marked intentionally out of scope.
5. Who reveals actors, stakeholders, audiences, authority, and responsibility.
6. What defines the object of explanation.
7. When establishes timing, sequence, duration, history, and freshness.
8. Where clarifies place, setting, jurisdiction, scale, and system boundary.
9. Why should distinguish cause, purpose, rationale, significance, and values.
10. How should explain mechanism, method, workflow, implementation, or maintenance.
