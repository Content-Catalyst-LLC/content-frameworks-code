# Method Notes: STP as Content-Framework Practice

STP is used here as a communication-governance framework.

## Segmentation

A segment should represent a meaningful difference that changes communication decisions: examples, evidence, tone, structure, distribution, or pathway design.

## Targeting

Targeting should be transparent. A segment may be prioritized because of need intensity, strategic fit, reachability, evidence fit, or ethical responsibility.

## Positioning

Positioning should clarify category, audience, problem, differentiation, proof, and limits. It should not inflate uniqueness.

## Ethical review

STP should be reviewed for stereotype risk, exclusion risk, weak evidence, and manipulative framing.
