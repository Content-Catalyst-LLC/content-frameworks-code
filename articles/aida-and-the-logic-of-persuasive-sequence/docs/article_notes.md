# Article Notes

AIDA and the Logic of Persuasive Sequence follows **Interdisciplinary Frameworks and Knowledge Bridges** and precedes **PAS, BAB, and the Structure of Tension and Transformation**.

The companion code models AIDA as a governed persuasive-sequence framework: attention, interest, desire, action, stage balance, claim support, call-to-action clarity, ethical-risk review, audience agency, and revision queues.
