# Modeling Principles

1. Content audits should evaluate structure, not just page counts.
2. Governance should connect audit findings to editorial decisions.
3. Metadata fields should support maintenance, not decoration.
4. Published articles should meet minimum standards for metadata, links, evidence, freshness, accessibility, and governance notes.
5. Planned articles should remain visible in the article map so coverage gaps can be governed.
6. Internal-link health should be treated as part of framework health.
7. Evidence readiness should include references, evidence notes, and limitations.
8. Freshness should depend on article type and review cycle, not date alone.
9. Framework-health scores should guide review, not replace editorial judgment.
10. Catalyst Canvas-style workflows should produce reproducible audit trails and catalog exports.
