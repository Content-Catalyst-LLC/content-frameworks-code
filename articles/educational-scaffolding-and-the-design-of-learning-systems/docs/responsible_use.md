# Responsible Use

These workflows are editorial and learning-design support tools.

Use them to:

- identify weak learning pathways
- inspect prerequisite gaps
- evaluate concept coverage
- flag high cognitive-load risk
- find missing examples or feedback prompts
- review transfer support
- inspect accessibility scaffolds
- generate governance review queues

Do not use scaffold-readiness scores as proof of learning, teaching quality, learner outcomes, accessibility compliance, or conceptual mastery. Human educational and editorial judgment remains necessary.
