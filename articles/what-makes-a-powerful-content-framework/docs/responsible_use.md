# Responsible Use

Framework-quality workflows support editorial review. They do not replace editorial judgment.

Use them to:

- compare frameworks across domains
- identify weak quality dimensions
- flag ethical and evidence risks
- generate governance queues
- support content-system maintenance
- prepare framework libraries for product use

Do not use scores as final truth. A framework with a high score may still be inappropriate in a specific context. A framework with a lower score may be useful if its scope is narrow and its limits are clear.
