# Article Notes

## Article

What Makes a Powerful Content Framework?

## Series Role

This is the third article in the Content Frameworks series. It follows:

1. What Are Content Frameworks?
2. Why Frameworks Matter in Research, Education, and Strategic Communication

It precedes:

4. Framework Literacy and the Structure of Usable Knowledge

## Core Function

This article defines the quality criteria for strong content frameworks, including clarity, coherence, transferability, adaptability, explanatory depth, domain fit, audience fit, evidence alignment, ethical use, and governance.
