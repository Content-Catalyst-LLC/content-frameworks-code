# Responsible Use

Use these workflows to identify weak narrative structure, missing context, unsupported transformation claims, agency distortion, overdramatized tension, savior framing, unsupported anecdotes, pressure CTAs, and governance gaps.

Do not use storytelling readiness scores as proof that a story is ethical, representative, accurate, or effective. The outputs support editorial review; they do not replace evidence assessment, audience research, accessibility review, consent review, or ethical judgment.
