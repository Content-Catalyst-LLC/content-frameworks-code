# Content Frameworks: Structure, Strategy, and Knowledge Systems

This folder supports the Content Frameworks pillar with reproducible examples for:

- framework-library comparison
- pillar and topic-cluster mapping
- internal-link graph analysis
- content audit scoring
- editorial metadata modeling
- taxonomy and framework classification
- SQL content-system schemas
- reusable content templates

Important distinction: content frameworks are not merely templates. They are structured models that organize how ideas are introduced, sequenced, connected, and made usable.
