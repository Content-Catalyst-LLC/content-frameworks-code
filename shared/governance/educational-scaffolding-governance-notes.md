# Educational Scaffolding Governance Notes

A learning-system audit should ask:

- Are foundational articles available before advanced articles?
- Are prerequisites visible?
- Are examples close to the concepts they support?
- Are feedback prompts available?
- Does the article support transfer?
- Is cognitive load managed?
- Are headings, links, alt text, and summaries accessible?
- Are planned articles creating gaps in the learning pathway?
- Does the scaffold preserve learner agency?
