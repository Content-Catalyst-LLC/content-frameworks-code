# Content Audit Governance Notes

Content frameworks should be audited for:

- inventory accuracy
- article-map coverage
- planned vs published gaps
- metadata completeness
- repository-link accuracy
- internal-link health
- evidence and references
- limitation statements
- accessibility readiness
- freshness and review cycles
- duplicate or overlap risk
- taxonomy drift
- framework-health score
- governance queue status

Audit metrics identify where editorial judgment is needed. They do not replace judgment.
