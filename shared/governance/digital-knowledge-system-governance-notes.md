# Digital Knowledge System Governance Notes

Digital knowledge-system reviews should check:

- whether content records have clear roles
- whether taxonomy categories remain coherent
- whether metadata fields are complete
- whether internal links support reader movement
- whether repositories match published articles
- whether references and evidence remain current
- whether accessibility metadata is complete
- whether review dates are current
- whether AI-generated classifications or metadata require review
- whether outdated records should be revised, merged, or retired
