# Taxonomy Design Governance Notes

Taxonomy reviews should check:

- whether each article has one primary category
- whether secondary categories and facets are useful
- whether tags are controlled
- whether active categories have enough content or a clear planned role
- whether category boundaries remain clear
- whether deprecated labels are still being used
- whether category names remain reader-friendly
- whether taxonomy metadata is complete
- whether AI-assisted labels require review
- whether categories should be merged, renamed, deprecated, or retired
