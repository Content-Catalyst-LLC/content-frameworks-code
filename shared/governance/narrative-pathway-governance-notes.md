# Narrative Pathway Governance Notes

Narrative pathway reviews should check:

- whether readers can orient within the larger series
- whether each article has a clear article role and pathway role
- whether prerequisites are visible
- whether next-step links are meaningful
- whether bridge transitions explain why movement matters
- whether internal links serve reader understanding
- whether metadata is complete
- whether planned articles still belong in the pathway
- whether footer navigation matches the article map
- whether pathways are reviewed as the system grows
